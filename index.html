My Work

Brooke Babb

Hi! Thanks for stopping by. I’m an ambitious and curious Junior UX/UI Designer who never gives up and loves helping people achieve their goals with user-center design. My ever-expanding toolbox to help connect and understand people includes my UX/UI certification of professional education at Georgia Institute of Technology along with 10 years in event design which showcased skills including problem solving, logical reasoning and empathizing with users.

I discovered my UX/UI calling when I was helping my entrepreneur fiancé with his side gigs and was conducting usability testing and task analysis, and he asked if I had ever heard of user experience or user interface. It was then that I dove right in and discovered my love for user research and design.

One of the things I love about life, is that you never know where it will take you.

A little bit about me personally is that I’m always trying to grow and learn something new, as my favorite question is “why?”. Outside of work I love fishing, campaign, hiking, traveling or dancing. Pretty much anything that is active and gets me out to enjoy life with others.

I am currently looking for new opportunities, so please don't hesitate to reach out— I'm happy to chat about work or play!
